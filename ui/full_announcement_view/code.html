H-1B Visa Application Deadlines Officially Extended for 2024

calendar_month October 24, 2023
schedule 10:00 AM EST
verified Immigration Admin Team

Reference ID: #ANN-2023-894

We are writing to inform all registered agencies and legal professionals of a significant update regarding the H-1B visa application process for the upcoming fiscal year. Due to unforeseen processing delays and scheduled system maintenance, the USCIS has officially extended the registration and application deadlines.

Key Changes to the Timeline

The initial registration period, which was set to close on November 1st, has been pushed back by two weeks. Please note the following critical dates for your case files:

  • check
    New Registration Deadline: November 15, 2023 at 12:00 PM EST
  • check
    Lottery Results Announcement: December 1, 2023 (Estimated)
  • check
    Petition Filing Window: December 5, 2023 – March 5, 2024

This extension is intended to allow ample time for all representatives to ensure their client data is accurate within the new portal system. The administration recommends reviewing all draft applications before the new deadline.

Required Actions

Agencies must re-verify the beneficiary details if they were entered prior to October 20th due to the database migration. No action is needed for applications started after this date.

For technical support regarding the submission portal, please reach out to our dedicated help desk or consult the updated FAQ section.
