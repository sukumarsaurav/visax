# Immizy — Keyword Strategy (India → Abroad)

> Last updated: 2026-05-23.
> Volume / intent estimates are heuristic — verify with Ahrefs/SEMrush/Google Keyword Planner before allocating budget.

---

## 1. The funnel

We bucket keywords by intent so each page targets one job:

| Intent | Stage | Page type | Conversion goal |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Informational** | Research | `/immigration/:destination`, blog | Email capture, "find consultant" CTA |
| **Comparison** | Evaluation | `/compare/canada-vs-australia`, `/compare/germany-vs-uk` | Land on destination page → CTA |
| **Transactional** | Decision | `/find-professionals`, `/consultant/:id`, city pages | Book consultation |
| **Brand/Service** | Hire | `/services/:slug`, agency pages | Direct booking |
| **Long-tail "X for Y"** | High-intent | `/immigration/canada-pr-for-software-engineer` | Book consultation |
